Join Us!

Whether you are an API maniac or you like to get your hands dirty on the Chrome DevTools, trying to optimize a bunch of JavaScript code, if you are passionate and enthusiast about what you’re doing…then we should have a chat!

At Namshi we are always looking for talented individuals who want to strive towards clean code, design patterns and want to use the latest technologies in their daily work — ever heard of SPDY, WebP, AngularJS or Redis? These are just a few of the things we use at Namshi.

About our stack

On the backend, we like to use NodeJS, Symfony2 and have based our Service Oriented Architecture on “RESTish” APIs, while, at the front, we’ve decided to fight the battle with AngularJS and Javascript in general (gulp, browserify and all the new kids on the block).
